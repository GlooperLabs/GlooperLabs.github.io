About Glooper

Glooper Labs is a research led company that specialises in the development and implementation of mathematical models for dynamic systems. Originally formed out of the Entrepreneur first incubator in London, our initial projects focussed around financial modelling, identifying trends, and estimating company perfomance.

Nowadays Glooper acts as an intermeditary between academia and industry with regards to the application of statistical methodology. In particular, we are active contributors and developers of open-source software for the analysis of dynamic systems. At Glooper we aim to help tackle some of the worlds most interesting challenges, from detecting cyber-attacks, to understanding the circuitary of the brain.
